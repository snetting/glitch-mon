Leaked Words
When a statistical glitch is detected, the client scans the same rolling bit buffer as ASCII across bit shifts, inverted bytes, bit-reversed bytes, and backwards byte order. Any 3+ letter dictionary matches are reported here as leaked words. Short matches are common in random data, so the client filters dictionary noise and reports the longest matches first.

Monobit (Entropy Bias)

Measures the statistical balance of binary states. A significant deviation indicates a distributional bias in the local entropy stream. In this experiment, we monitor these "glitches" to investigate if the fundamental randomness of reality is influenced by—or correlates with—significant real-world events and local environmental shifts.

Runs (Pattern Detection)

Analyzes the sequence of identical outcomes to detect emergent structure. While a source may appear balanced, the Runs test identifies hidden order or oscillations. We track these patterns globally to see if the "matrix" of local randomness occasionally exhibits non-local synchronization during moments of high collective impact.
